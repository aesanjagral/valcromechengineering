Who We Are

Leading provider of mechanical seals and industrial solutions in India

VELCROMECH ENGINEERING was established in 2010 with a vision to provide premium quality mechanical seals and industrial solutions to various industries across India. Over the years, we have built a reputation for excellence, reliability, and innovation in the field of sealing technology.

Our company specializes in a comprehensive range of mechanical seals including single spring seals, multi spring seals, cartridge seals, agitator seals, bellow seals, and industrial pumps. We serve diverse industries including chemical, pharmaceutical, textile, food & beverage, oil & gas, and water treatment sectors.

With our team of experienced engineers and technicians, we not only supply high-quality products but also provide comprehensive technical support, installation services, maintenance programs, and training to ensure optimal performance of your equipment.
