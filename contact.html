Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries

What is your response time for technical support?

We provide 24/7 emergency support with response times of 2-4 hours for critical issues and same-day response for general inquiries during business hours.

Do you provide on-site installation services?

Yes, we offer professional on-site installation and commissioning services across India. Our certified technicians ensure proper installation and optimal performance.

What warranty do you provide on your products?

We provide comprehensive warranty coverage ranging from 12 to 24 months depending on the product. All warranty terms include parts replacement and technical support.

Can you customize seals for specific applications?

Absolutely! We specialize in custom seal design and manufacturing for unique applications. Our engineering team works closely with clients to develop tailored solutions.
